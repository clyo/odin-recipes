Cheeseburger Recipe


This is a recipe for a juicy cheeseburger

Ingredients
  • Ground Beef
  • Oil
  • American Cheese
  • Buns
  • Condiments

  1. Take 0.3lbs of ground beef and mould it into a patty
  2. place it on a hot skillet with oil already added
  3. With spatula push the burger down and let cook
  4. After 5 minutes flip the burger and push down again with spatula
  5. Add cheese on top of patty
  6. Add the cheese patty to a bun and voila
